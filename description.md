## Problem Background
Mr. Andreas is a 40-year-old family man with two children planning to purchase a house in the Northeast States, USA. With a budget of **$2,000,000 USD**, he needs data-driven insights to make a confident housing decision that fits both his financial constraints and family needs.

## Project Output
An interactive Jupyter Notebook containing:
- Distribution analysis of housing prices
- Outlier detection and handling
- Budget feasibility analysis
- Feature impact visualizations (area, bedrooms, stories, amenities)
- A comprehensive summary dashboard

## Data
Dataset sourced from [Kaggle.com](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/yasserh/housing-prices-dataset) — Housing Prices Dataset  
- **545 rows × 13 columns**
- Features: price, area, bedrooms, bathrooms, stories, mainroad, guestroom, basement, hotwaterheating, airconditioning, parking, prefarea, furnishingstatus

## Stacks
`pandas` · `numpy` · `scipy` · `matplotlib` · `nbformat`
